Who We Are

VP Informatics, Inc. is an IT consulting company specializing in scientific data management and personalized IT support, with a focus on early drug discovery and pre-clinical research. For over 25 years we have been helping scientists navigate through the ocean of information generated by their cutting-edge work, allowing clients to focus on making their next big discovery without getting bogged down by the data.

Learn more

Our Mission

We strive to give scientists access to clean data. This empowers our customers to spend their time understanding the data rather than managing and troubleshooting software. On the data management side, we simplify and streamline applications, integrate data flows, and provide reliable data. On the IT consulting side, we engage in active interaction with users, providing essential user training and support. User feedback is extremely important for us to optimize our strategies.
